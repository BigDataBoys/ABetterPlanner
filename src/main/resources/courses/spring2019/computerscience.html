Important Academic Information

Effective January 1, 2018 all CSU campuses are required to identify course sections that exclusively use digital course materials that are free of charge to students and which may have lost-cost print options. Sections meeting these qualifications are identified by the respective academic department and are referred to as Zero Cost Course Material (ZCCM) sections. You may search for ZCCM courses by choosing "Zero Cost Course Materials" from Course Attribute search criteria. Please refer to https://als.csuprojects.org/legislation for more information on the Zero Cost Course Material (ZCCM).
