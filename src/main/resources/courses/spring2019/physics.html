Important Academic Information

Sections meeting these qualifications are identified by the respective academic department and are referred to as Zero Cost Course Material (ZCCM) sections. You may search for ZCCM courses by choosing "Zero Cost Course Materials" from Course Attribute search criteria. Please refer to https://als.csuprojects.org/legislation for more information on the Zero Cost Course Material (ZCCM).
